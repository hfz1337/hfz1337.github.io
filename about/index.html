About

You really want to know who I am?

# whoami

My name is Hafidh, I’m currently studying Computer Science at the Higher National School of Computer Science (Algiers). (ESI ex. INI)
I’m passionate about Cyber Security and everything that involves understanding the inner workings of things and breaking them.

CTF player

I started playing CTFs1 since I got introduced into the amazing field of Cyber Security. I was skipping studies and homeworks to play CTFs whenever I could, this became an essential part of my life as it teaches a lot about Information Security and other esoteric topics.
The funny thing about this field is that the more you learn new things, the more it becomes appetizing.
I’m currently playing with the Algerian CTF team Sudo_root.

Workshop instructor

Shellmates club is a group of students passionate about Information Security, they take care of BSides Algiers since 2011.
I joined this club slightly before my first year in college after seeing the events they organize and how cool the community was. This is when my journey started with Cyber Security.
I now see it as a duty to give back what I have been taught by old members to the new ones. I do this by animating talks/workshops in different Cyber Security topics and organizing related events with my fellow members, mostly CTF competitions and Cyber Security awareness events to raise the public’s awareness and teach them how to stay safe online.

Skills

I’m a motivated person driven by curiosity. I try to be good at every aspect of Cyber Security to be prepared for tomorrow’s industry.
My favorite scripting language is Python, I use it daily to automate boring tasks. (sometimes not related to computer sience nor cyber security at all)
You can find me on different Cyber Security training platforms like Root Me and Hack The Box.

About this blog

I never liked web design. In fact, I never tried to learn how to design websites efficiently nor was attracted by this subject.
This blog was made during the self-quarantine period due to the COVID-192‘s outbreak. I tried to turn that period of crisis into productivity, so I decided to set up my own blog!
I used to write my articles in medium before, but I always wanted to have my own blog.
This is when a friend of mine introduced me to Hugo, which is a really cool framework for building websites with minimal efforts.
I used Hugo along with the Blackburn theme and added some personal modifications.
